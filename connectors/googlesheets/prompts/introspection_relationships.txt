Google Sheets is connected. Treat Google Sheets metadata tables as ambiguous.
For Google Sheets dynamic worksheet tables, read sheet_structure before suggesting relationships.
If sheet_structure.relationship_use is blocked, do not suggest relationships using that worksheet table; add a warning instead.
For Google Sheets data tables, data_samples may contain rows reconstructed from googlesheets_cell. Treat A/B/C keys and observed_first_row_values as cell values, not field names.
A named Google Sheets worksheet table is eligible for relationship suggestions only after sheet_structure.configured_header_row is set.
For Google Sheets worksheet tables, configured_header_values and configured_header_columns are the user-selected field names; exposed_columns must align to those headers.
Never suggest a Google Sheets relationship from a cell value, observed_first_row_values value, or unexpected_semantic_columns entry.
Only suggest Google Sheets relationship candidates when a worksheet table has configured_header_row and the table and column semantics are explicit enough for user review.
